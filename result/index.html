Music Style Transfer Demo

This site demonstrate the result of composition style transfer. Given an existing song, we aim to
generate a new music by changing the instrumentation and rearranging the music piece.

Strings: Violin + Cello / Band: Guitar + Bass + Melody

In this page, we provide some samples to demonstrate our music transcription result proposed by this paper: https://arxiv.org/pdf/1905.13567.pdf
